• Lemose Theresa Omotunde Modupeola

    was born on August 29, 1961, just outside the Sodeke compound in Abeokuta, Ogun State to Mr. & Mrs. Joseph Olayiwola Sodeke.

    She was the first of 5 children.

    Her parents worked diligently in the Nigerian civil service.

    She attended Ebenezer Grammar School, Iberekodo, Abeokuta, Ogun state.

  • She immediately followed the precedent set by her parents into the Civil service where she worked at the Ministry of Justice.

    Later on, she discovered she had a strong passion for education which propelled her to join the Egbado Teacher Training College, Ilaro, Ogun State.

    After bagging her teaching degree,She worked as an Educator for 35 years in the Nigerian civil service until she eventually retired gracefully and with Pride at the Owu Baptist Primary School in 2017.

    She was married to Alhaji Nojeem Ayobamidele Lemose.

  • She went on the holy pilgrimage to make the hajj in the holy city of Mecca in 2005, where she returned with the title of respect popularly known as Alhaja and a bright sparkling silver tooth.

    Alhaja Lemose Theresa Omotunde Modupeola is survived by her siblings, husband, and children-Titilayo, Wuraola & Oreoluwa.

    She had a limited range of hobbies which included but was not limited to partying, she was a fashionista and the life of the party, She was a music superfan, always listening and gyrating to the music of Wasiu Ayinde, Chief Ebenezer Obey, and the young stars like Davido and Wizkid.

  • She also loved passing time with movies and anticipating new releases, especially the ones that feature Sola Shobowale & Funke Akindele.

    She would drop positive comments on Sola Sobowale’s Facebook fan pages, and lastly, she loved reading the bible.

    She was a jovial person and a beacon of light to those who were privileged enough to experience her.

  • The panic of growing old was never a thought because even at 3scores and one, she was forever young and death seemed like a myth.

    Lemose Theresa Omotunde Modupeopla passed away onMonday, July 17, 2023, at the age of 61 years.

    She was a great woman, a respectful daughter, an active sister, a loving wife, and a dedicated and proud mother.

    I think we speak for everyone here when we say we were lucky to know her and she’ll be greatly missed by everyone she touched.
